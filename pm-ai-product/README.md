# pm-ai-product

AI product management skills for PMs building AI-powered products. Covers the full lifecycle from discovering where AI adds value, through strategy and design, to evaluation and responsible launch.

## Skills (14)

- **ai-use-case-mapping** — Map product features to AI capabilities, identify where AI adds real value vs. traditional approaches.
- **ai-feasibility-assessment** — Assess technical feasibility: model capabilities, data requirements, infrastructure needs, and timeline.
- **ai-data-readiness** — Audit data availability, quality, labeling pipeline, and privacy compliance for AI features.
- **ai-product-strategy** — Define AI product vision, differentiation, and competitive moat through AI capabilities.
- **ai-build-vs-buy** — Framework for build custom model vs. use API vs. fine-tune vs. open-source decisions.
- **ai-model-selection** — Compare and select AI models/providers for specific use cases with trade-off analysis.
- **ai-cost-modeling** — Model AI economics: token costs, compute, storage, scaling curves, and unit economics.
- **ai-prompt-strategy** — Design prompt architecture, versioning, testing, and optimization for LLM-powered products.
- **ai-ux-patterns** — Design UX for AI uncertainty, latency, streaming, error states, and graceful degradation.
- **ai-human-in-the-loop** — Design human oversight, escalation paths, feedback loops, and quality control systems.
- **ai-responsible-design** — Ethics, bias detection, fairness, transparency, and safety guardrails for AI features.
- **ai-risk-assessment** — Identify and mitigate AI-specific risks: hallucination, adversarial attacks, data leaks, model drift.
- **ai-evaluation-framework** — Design metrics and eval pipelines for AI quality: accuracy, relevance, safety, latency.
- **ai-launch-readiness** — Pre-launch checklist for AI features: quality gates, monitoring, rollback, and scaling plan.

## Commands (5)

- `/pm-ai-product:ai-discover` — Discover and validate AI use cases: map opportunities, assess feasibility, and audit data readiness.
- `/pm-ai-product:ai-strategy` — AI product strategy: vision, build-vs-buy, model selection, and cost modeling.
- `/pm-ai-product:ai-design` — Design AI experiences: prompt architecture, UX patterns, and human-in-the-loop systems.
- `/pm-ai-product:ai-responsible` — Responsible AI: ethics review, bias detection, risk assessment, and safety guardrails.
- `/pm-ai-product:ai-evaluate` — AI quality evaluation: metrics framework, eval pipelines, and launch readiness checklist.
